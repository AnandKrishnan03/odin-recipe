potato wedges

Crispy Garlic Baked Potato Wedges are soft pillows on
the inside, crunchy on the outside with a good kick
of garlic and parmesan cheese, and NO pre-soaking or
parboiling needed!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes - 3 large
  • Cooking oil - 3 tablespoon
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Pepper - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garlic powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Dried oregano - 1 tablespoon
  • Paprika - 1 tablespoon
  • Parmesan cheese -


Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).


  2. Thoroughly wash potatoes, cut in half, and slice into wedges.


  3. Toss wedges in cooking oil and seasonings.


  4. Place on a baking sheet, skin side down.


  5. Bake 40-50 minutes.


  6. Enjoy!
